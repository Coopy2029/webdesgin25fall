This website is designed to promote the Basketball Leadership Summit 2025, a professional event focused on developing basketball skills, leadership, and community. The page features a clean layout with a navigation bar that allows users to easily access sections such as Home, About, Event Details, and Speakers. A large hero section introduces the summit, highlighting its reputation and purpose, along with a call-to-action button encouraging visitors to register. The About section explains the challenges and growth opportunities within basketball, while the Event Details section clearly presents the schedule and venue information using organized cards. The Speakers section showcases key coaches, trainers, and players with images and descriptions, helping visitors connect with the people leading the summit. Overall, the website combines informative content, visuals, and structured sections to effectively communicate the importance and excitement of the event.
