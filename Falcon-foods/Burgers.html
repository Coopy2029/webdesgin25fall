    • How To Make A Burger

    To make a burger you will need the following ingredients:

  • ground beef, salt, pepper, burger buns, and your choice of toppings.
  • First, you will need to form the ground beef into patties. Make sure to season the patties with salt and pepper on both sides.
  • Next, heat a grill or a skillet over medium-high heat.
  • Once the grill or skillet is hot, place the patties on it and cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side for medium-rare burgers. If you prefer your burgers more well-done, cook them for an additional 1-2 minutes on each side.
  • While the burgers are cooking, you can toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  • Once the burgers are cooked to your desired level of doneness, remove them from the grill or skillet and let them rest for a few minutes. Finally, assemble your burger by placing the patty on the bottom bun and adding your choice of toppings
