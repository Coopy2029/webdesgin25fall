About Brad Pitt

Brad Pitt, born William Bradley Pitt on December 18, 1963, is an American actor and producer. He has achieved global fame with his versatility in films spanning a variety of genres. He is one of the highest-grossing actors in Hollywood history.

Brad is known for his roles in films like "Fight Club," "Inglourious Basterds," and "Once Upon a Time in Hollywood." He is also recognized for his humanitarian work, founding the Make It Right foundation for rebuilding New Orleans after Hurricane Katrina.
